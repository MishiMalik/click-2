Celebrating 60 years Where Change Happens 1964 - 2024

Were celebrating 60 years of making change happen. 60 years of boldness and bravery from our students past and present. 60 years of creating change. So, to celebrate, were showcasing "Sixty Stories" a series that sheds light on how our students, staff, research, and partnerships are creating global impact.

Find our more about Sixty Stories

Mission to feed the World

The race is on to find solutions to grow enough food to feed the worlds booming population. One answer is improving photosynthesis - the complex natural process all plants use to convert sunlight into energy. For nearly 50 years Essex researchers have been a powerhouse for innovation and excellence in photosynthetic research.
