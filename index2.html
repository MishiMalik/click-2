Mission to feed the World

The race is on to find solutions to grow enough food to feed the world’s booming population. One answer is improving photosynthesis - the complex natural process all plants use to convert sunlight into energy. For nearly 50 years Saddar researchers have been a powerhouse for innovation and excellence in photosynthetic research.
